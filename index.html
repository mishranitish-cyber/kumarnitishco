About Kumar Nitish & Co

KUMAR NITISH & CO is a Chartered Accountancy Firm committed to delivering high-quality professional services with integrity, excellence and client-focused solutions.

The firm specializes in Taxation, GST Compliance, Audit & Assurance, Accounting Services, Corporate Compliance, Financial Reporting, Business Advisory and Banking Consultancy.

We aim to provide practical and result-oriented solutions that help businesses achieve regulatory compliance, financial discipline and sustainable growth.

About the Proprietor

CA Nitish Kumar is a qualified Chartered Accountant and JAIIB-certified banking professional with rich experience across the banking and financial services sector.
