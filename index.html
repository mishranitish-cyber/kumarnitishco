About The Firm

KUMAR NITISH & CO is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm committed to delivering trusted financial solutions with integrity, professionalism and excellence.

The firm provides comprehensive services in Audit & Assurance, Taxation, GST Compliance, Accounting, Corporate Compliance, Financial Reporting, Banking Consultancy and Business Advisory.

We work closely with businesses, startups, professionals and individuals to provide practical solutions, ensure regulatory compliance and support sustainable growth.

About the Founder

CA Nitish Kumar (B.Com, ACA, JAIIB)

CA Nitish Kumar is a qualified Chartered Accountant and JAIIB-certified banking professional with experience across banking, finance, taxation and business advisory services.

Prior to establishing KUMAR NITISH & CO, he worked with leading financial institutions including HDFC Bank and Yes Bank, gaining valuable experience in credit appraisal, financial analysis, banking operations, risk management and regulatory compliance.
