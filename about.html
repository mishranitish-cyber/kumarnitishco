About KUMAR NITISH & CO

KUMAR NITISH & CO is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm committed to delivering trusted financial solutions with integrity, professionalism, and excellence. Established with the vision of providing high-quality professional services to businesses, startups, professionals, and individuals, the firm offers comprehensive solutions in the areas of Audit & Assurance, Taxation, GST Compliance, Accounting, Corporate Compliance, Financial Reporting, Banking Consultancy, and Business Advisory. At KUMAR NITISH & CO, we believe that professional excellence is built on a foundation of technical competence, ethical conduct, client-centric service, and continuous learning. Our approach goes beyond traditional compliance services. We work closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, identify opportunities, manage risks, and provide practical solutions that contribute to sustainable growth and long-term success. .
