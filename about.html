About KUMAR NITISH & CO

KUMAR NITISH & CO is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm committed to delivering trusted financial solutions with integrity, professionalism, and excellence. Established with the vision of providing high-quality professional services to businesses, startups, professionals, and individuals, the firm offers comprehensive solutions in the areas of Audit & Assurance, Taxation, GST Compliance, Accounting, Corporate Compliance, Financial Reporting, Banking Consultancy, and Business Advisory. At KUMAR NITISH & CO, we believe that professional excellence is built on a foundation of technical competence, ethical conduct, client-centric service, and continuous learning. Our approach goes beyond traditional compliance services. We work closely with our clients to understand their business objectives, identify opportunities, manage risks, and provide practical solutions that contribute to sustainable growth and long-term success. The firm is led by CA Nitish Kumar (B.Com, ACA, JAIIB), a qualified Chartered Accountant and banking professional with extensive experience in the financial services sector. Having worked with leading banking institutions such as HDFC Bank and Yes Bank, he brings valuable expertise in financial analysis, credit appraisal, banking operations, risk management, regulatory compliance, and business advisory. This unique combination of banking and professional accountancy experience enables the firm to provide insightful and commercially relevant solutions to clients across diverse industries. Our service portfolio includes Income Tax Return Filing, Tax Planning, GST Registration and Compliance, Accounting and Bookkeeping Services, Audit & Assurance Services, ROC Compliance, Startup Advisory, Project Reports, CMA Data Preparation, Loan Proposal Assistance, Financial Analysis, and Business Consultancy. We strive to deliver timely, accurate, and practical advice that helps our clients meet regulatory requirements while achieving their strategic business goals. At KUMAR NITISH & CO, we are committed to building long-term relationships based on trust, transparency, and professional excellence. Whether you are a startup entrepreneur, a growing business, an established enterprise, or an individual seeking professional guidance, our objective is to be your trusted partner in navigating the complexities of finance, taxation, compliance, and business growth. Our mission is to empower clients with reliable financial insights, proactive compliance support, and strategic advisory services that create lasting value. Through dedication, integrity, and a client-first approach, we aim to contribute meaningfully to the success and growth of every client we serve. .

The firm provides a comprehensive range of professional services including Audit & Assurance, Income Tax, GST Compliance, Accounting, Financial Reporting, Corporate Compliance, Banking Consultancy and Business Advisory.
